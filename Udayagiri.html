Udayagiri Fort

  • Udayagiri Fort is known for its sprawling layout and its location atop a hill.
  • It has historical connections to the Travancore royal family and served as a defensive fortification.
  • The fort area also houses a temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara.
  • Udayagiri Fort was strategically located to protect the southern borders of the Travancore kingdom.
  • It was used as a military outpost and played a role in safeguarding the kingdom from external threats.
  • The fort offers a serene atmosphere and is an ideal spot for those interested in history and architecture.
