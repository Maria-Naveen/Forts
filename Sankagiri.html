Sankagiri Fort

  • Sankagiri Fort is known for its underground passages and chambers, which were used for storing ammunition and for escape during sieges.
  • The fort's architecture reflects a mix of Indian and European styles, showcasing its historical significance.
  • It is a popular spot for trekking and offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Sankagiri Fort is known for its role in the Anglo-Mysore Wars and the resistance against British forces.
  • It features a temple, granary, and a tunnel system that was used for storage and escape during sieges.
  • The fort provides a glimpse into the military history of the region.
