Namakkal Fort

  • The fort is characterized by its impressive rock-cut architecture and has a temple dedicated to Lord Narasimha (an incarnation of Lord Vishnu).
  • Namakkal Fort is known for its unique lion sculptures carved out of single stone pillars.
  • The fort offers a panoramic view of the town and its surroundings.
  • The fort also houses a large granary and a reservoir.
  • The rock formations inside the fort have intricate carvings depicting various deities and mythological scenes.
  • The fort's main attraction is the Namakkal Anjaneyar Temple, dedicated to Lord Hanuman. It is a prominent pilgrimage site.
